Meet the Families You’re Helping.

Fiona*, a student in Malawi, used to miss school every time she had her period — because there was only one latrine for 749 girls and almost no privacy.

Thanks to supporters like you, her school built new private bathrooms. She hasn’t missed a day of school since.

Millions of girls face similar challenges, but with clean water and menstrual health education, that’s changing — one story, one school, and one brave girl at a time.
